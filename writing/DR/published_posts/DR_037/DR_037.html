On margin plummeting with adulthood

E037|17.05.25

I notice since the age of 18 or around the time when I joined upper high school, the older I've gotten, the more costly my misbehavior has become. It's like I would slack off and decide not to study in lower levels of school without much repurcution but now it feels like there's so much to lose. It feels like its not only me that's affected by my mistakes. It also feels like I feel the mistakes more, probably because of the prior mentioned increased effect. Could actually be an actual thing that, the deeper we sink into adulthood, the less margin we have for making mistakes. Something worth thinking about when deciding what to spend your time, choosing if to take the path of least resistance or put in the reps in whichever life domain you are trying to improve.
