On focusing on what we have, what we can do that we haven't done.

E038|18.05.25

It's not about if you have resources. It's not about if you have all the connections. It's about if you are to the fullest of what you currently can using all you have available to you. If you are every passing day through practice and exploration increasing the level of how much you can get yourself to do.
