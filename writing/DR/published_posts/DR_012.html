The best stress pill 💊 --> Taking action.

E012 | 22.04.25

From experience and listening to others share on the same issue, I strongly believe avoiding taking action that we know we should is a major cause of stress. Of worry.

The accumulated list of things we ought to do that we keep pushing forward to the future for all the various reasons we have, weighs down on us in form of the thoughts about the real consequences that'll ensue from the bad decisions of procrastination.

And it is this worry, whose cause is easy to pin point most times, that builds up into stress. Stress which, at least for most of the times, we find it hard to know what is causing it.

It's interesting how a little bit of introspection, listing down all you have to that you can do but haven't done and, item by item clearing the list can make.

It's interesting how procrastination (delaying necessary possible action) leads to worry and then to stress. How reverse engineering this and taking action can solve stress whose cause we did not know was un taken action.
