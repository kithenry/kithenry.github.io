On the paradox of finding challenge with letting go

E040|21.05.25

This one will be raw thought. No attempt to provide a solution. Just dumping thought. It will for ever interest me why something that's so simple and equally as desirable can be to many (me included) if not most extremely hard. The as if innate drive to persistently attempt to puppeteer the future. Letting go..

Letting go should be easier I expected. I still wonder why it isn't. The paradox in the predisposition to hunger for control. Control mostly over what we can't. And frustratingly, what we know we can't. It's an interesting paradox is all I can say for now. I thought it would make more sense if more people needed advice on how to not let go, on how to keep on keeping on. But its the opposite. I find there's more people in need of 'please let go.. stop trying .. u can't do that .. focus on what you can do.. ' kind of advice.. Like.. really..

And even more interesting is that even when one trys to let go. They could end up for all their effort being frustrated they can't master 'letting go'. Chasing the wind of mastery. Can't let go of the fact they won't ever be fully detached from their natural predisposition.
